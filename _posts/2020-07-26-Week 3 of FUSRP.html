This week was a busy week. Prof.Hilfinger clarifies our research goals again. It is definitely more clear when things are written down as physically-seen words. I finally understand that we are trying to find a procedure to characterize individual, a fingerprint given one’s glucose data in the hope to provide personalized medicine service. To achieve this goal, we need to analyze the given data (box plots, autocorrelation, big drop & rise), while finding a control and regulation rule that explains how glucose data interacts in one’s body. We also have our midterm presentation soon. I found a template and tried to make slides look visually attractive. My teammates will be responsible for the intellectually attractive part :D

We have tried new ways to measure the interpolation error and the device error. Another week that I got carried by my research partner...

I found myself lack motivation while working remotely at home; really miss the days where we can go to library to study/work with friends. Lots of readings to do for lecture notes... The hardest part is to start doing it. Otherwise, I can procrastinate the whole day with no efforts :(
